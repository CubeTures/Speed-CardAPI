Controls

Press space to start the game.

The player on the left cycles cards using A and D and places a card using W.

The player on the right cycles cards using Left Arrow and Right Arrow and places a card using Up Arrow.

When no player has any moves left, the playing decks will automatically shuffle so that there is a viable play.

Want to play again? Press space!

Spacing

Game

This game replicates the playground ruleset for speed.

There are two playing piles which you play your cards on.

You can only play a card onto one of the piles if the card you have is one less or one more than the one in the pile.

For example, a 4 will play on 5, a Jack will play on 10, an Ace will play on King, and all vice versa.

Be the first one to play all your cards to win!
